Our Commitment to Child Safety

Preventing Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Zero Tolerance Policy

We strictly prohibit any content or behavior that involves or promotes CSAE, including:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Grooming or solicitation of minors
  • Sexualized depictions of minors
  • Attempts to bypass detection or reporting systems

Violations result in immediate suspension, reporting to authorities, and permanent removal.

Proactive Detection and Moderation

We use advanced tools and human moderation to detect and prevent CSAE-related content:

  • AI-based scanning of images, videos, and text
  • Hash-matching against CSAM databases (e.g., PhotoDNA)
  • Behavioral monitoring for grooming patterns

Reporting and Response

Users can report harmful content anonymously. We respond by:

  • Preserving evidence securely
  • Notifying law enforcement
  • Supporting affected individuals and families
