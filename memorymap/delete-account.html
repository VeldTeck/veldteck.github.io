Delete Your Memory Map Account

This page explains how to delete your account and associated data from Memory Map, developed by Memory Map Labs.

🧭 How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Open the Memory Map app, go to Settings > Account > Delete, and follow the prompts.

Option 2: Email us at support@memorymap.app with the subject line "Delete My Account" and include your account email.

🗑️ What Data Is Deleted

  • Your account profile (email, username, preferences)
  • Any personal notes or annotations

📁 What Data May Be Retained

  • Anonymous usage statistics (for app improvement)
  • Legal records if required by law

Retention Period: Backup data is retained for up to 30 days before permanent deletion.

🔒 Privacy Commitment

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have full control over your data. If you have questions, reach out anytime at support@memorymap.app.
