Maisonly.io — Marketplace for private chef experiences

Maisonly connects culinary students with people looking for private chef experiences. Students gain real-world opportunities and portfolio-building gigs; hosts enjoy curated in-home dining without restaurant overhead.

What Maisonly enables
  • Students: real-world gigs, income, and portfolio work.
  • Hosts: memorable private dining without restaurant overhead.
  • Community: local culinary talent on display.
